ABOUT

Matthew Kilgore has been the Tailor for The Drew Barrymore Show since the show began in 2020. He is responsible for all on-set tailoring for the show, as well as custom-built costumes. In addition to tailoring, he handles the wardrobe needs of each guest. He designed and built the iconic Glinda costume for Drew during season one, and recently recreated the M3GAN costume for a special segment with Allison Williams. Matthew, along with Drew's Stylist Lee Harris, and the entire Hair, Make-up, and Wardrobe team, won two Daytime Emmy Awards for the 2021-2022 season.

Matthew has worked on Broadway as a stitcher for Tina: The Tina Turner Musical and as a dresser for Hamilton, Frozen, and The Iceman Cometh. He designed and built costumes for Broadway Backwards, a fundraising concert for Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS. In early 2023 you can find his tailoring skills on the silver screen when the film Spinning Gold hits theaters. 

Matthew firmly believes that you feel best when your clothes fit. So, let's work together to perfect your fit.
